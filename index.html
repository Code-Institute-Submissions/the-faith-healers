BLUES - FUNK - ROCK - GOOD TIMES

We aim to do exactly what it says on the tin Our material spans classic to modern blues, funk, a bit of soul, and rock, with the aim to appeal to a wide audience. Two guitars, including slide, and blues harp in the mix gives us our great sound. The aim is always to deliver to the highest standard we can. Our favourite sight is people getting up to dance with a smile on their faces.
